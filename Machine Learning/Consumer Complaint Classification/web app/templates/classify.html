Hello. Welcome to Consumer Complaint Prediction Application!

    I. INTRODUCTION

    This project is to use Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing algorithms to train a classifier to classify consumer financial product complaint narratives into different product types, for example, is the complaint very likely to be mortgage-related issue? Is the complaint very likely to be credit card-related issue etc. A logistic regression model was trained on Consumer Complaint Database (2011 - 2017) First let us take a look at some exploratory data analysis.


    II. EXPLORATORY DATA ANALYSIS


    As the chart above (Figure 1) shows, mortgages received the most number of complaints in the period of 2011 - 2017.



    The heat map/correlations (Figure 3) show the following:

    1) Mortgage related complaints are generally submitted via referral, and they tend to be closed without relief, or to be closed with explanations;

    2) Credit card and bank account services related complaints are more likely to be closed with monetary relief
